Final Accounts

  • Boniface Kingori
  • Nov 10, 2022

The balance brought down from the ledger accounts they serve in helping to prepare the final accounts. Final account are final financial statements that are used in analyzing the performing of the business. They indicates the profits and ../assets of the business, this final financial statement includes the following:

a. Trading profit and loss account.
b. The balance sheet.

TRADING , PROFIT AND LOSS STATEMENT


Also referred to as the statement of financial performance/ income statement.
This statement seeks to report on the gross profit and net profit of a business.
The determination of these profits follows the formula below
SALES – COST OF GOODS SOLD = GROSS PROFIT + OTHER INCOMES – EXPENSE = NET PROFIT
Cost of goods sold = opening stock + purchases + carriage inwards – return outwards- closing stock

Balance Sheet

Also referred to as statement of financial position / position statement.
Reports on the position of ../assets of a business at a given point in time and also shows the total funding that has contributed to this ../assets.
This can be summarized into a regulation referred to as the accounting equation.
It is an equation that shows the total ../assets in one word and the total finding on the other i.e. total ../assets = total funding.
But total funding = owners funds (capital) + other sources of funds (liabilities).
The accounting equation is summarized as:

Total ../assets (A) = capital (C) + liabilities (L)
A= C + L (accounting equation)
