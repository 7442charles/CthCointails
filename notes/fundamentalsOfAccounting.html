Fundamentals of Accounting

  • Boniface Kingori
  • Nov 10, 2022

Accounting process through which financial information in a business is collected, processed and reported to the various users to facilitate their decision making concerning their interest to the business. This information is reported in financial reports referred to as financial statements. This statement reports about the financial performance and the financial position of a business at the end of the financial period. These financial statements include the following; -

Trading profit and loss statements: statements of financial performance. Balance sheet- assets/ statement of financial statement. The process through which accountants gather information about a business transaction then records and summarizes them in order to come up with the details represented in the financial system is referred to as accounting cycle.

Gross profit = selling price – buying price
